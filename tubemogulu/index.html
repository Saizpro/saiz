About TubeU

Now in its 4th year, TubeMogul University provides a unique opportunity to interact with a diverse group of global thought leaders and visionaries in the programmatic online video industry. Held annually in Australia, Europe and North America, the agenda for each event is focused on topics relating to advancements and trends in the programmatic industry combined with best practices for using programmatic software to achieve effective brand performance.
